#Thirsting Axe (#1085), sacrifice half: equipped bears deal no combat damage
#to a creature this turn -> sacrificed when my end step ends (asserted at the
#opponent's upkeep). Uses mytreason (controller's end step only).

firstmain
[PLAYER1]
manapool:{2}
inplay:thirsting axe,grizzly bears

thirsting axe
grizzly bears

goto end

[END]
