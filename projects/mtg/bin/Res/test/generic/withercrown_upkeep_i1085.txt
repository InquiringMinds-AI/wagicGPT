#Withercrown (#1085), upkeep leg: the enchanted creature's controller loses 1
#life unless they sacrifice it at THEIR upkeep. Crown their giant; they
#leave the pay prompt unanswered at their turn-2 upkeep (answering it with
#ANY choice index pays the 1 life and keeps the giant - that's the
#counterfactual) -> giant sacrificed, crown follows it to the graveyard. NOTE: this granted upcost WORKS because it rides a
#teach(creature) transforms - unlike the dead all(this)/lord grants
#(thirst, vapor snare, vile consumption) - the teach path wires AUpkeep.
[INIT]
firstmain
[PLAYER1]
manapool:{1}{B}
hand:withercrown
library:darksteel citadel,millstone
[PLAYER2]
inplay:hill giant
library:darksteel citadel,millstone
[DO]
withercrown
hill giant
goto firstmain
goto firstmain
goto end
goto upkeep
goto firstmain
goto firstmain
[ASSERT]
FIRSTMAIN
[PLAYER1]
graveyard:withercrown
library:darksteel citadel,millstone
[PLAYER2]
graveyard:hill giant
hand:*
library:darksteel citadel
[END]
